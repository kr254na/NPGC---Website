Privacy Statement

Last Updated: March 2026  |  National Post Graduate College, Lucknow

1. Introduction

National Post Graduate College (NPGC), Lucknow, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users who visit our website npgc.in. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard it.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and student registration details submitted through forms on our website.
  • Academic Information: Course enrollment, examination data, and results accessed through the college's academic portals.
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and pages visited, collected automatically through server logs and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing admission applications and student registrations.
  • Communicating important academic notices, examination schedules, and results.
  • Improving the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • Responding to queries and providing support through official communication channels.
  • Compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements as per the University of Lucknow and Government of Uttar Pradesh guidelines.

4. Data Security

NPGC takes reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized staff who require it to perform their duties. However, transmission of information over the internet cannot be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Sharing of Information

NPGC does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared with:

  • University of Lucknow − for academic record maintenance and affiliation compliance.
  • Government bodies − as required under applicable laws and regulations.
  • Examination boards − for conducting and certifying examinations.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device that help us understand how visitors use the site. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, this may affect the functionality of certain features of the website.

7. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. NPGC is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

8. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact: support@npgc.in
