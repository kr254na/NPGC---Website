Our Magazines

The college brings out the following regular publications aimed at showcasing student and faculty achievements, institutional milestones, and creative expressions.

Abhivyakti

Abhivyakti is the annual college magazine. Students and faculty members contribute to the publication by submitting their original writings, including poems, short stories, articles, news items, and material on other topics of interest.

The magazine also showcases various sports, co-curricular activities, and major events of the preceding year in the college through colored and monochrome photographs. Extraordinary achievements of the students, along with their photographs, are also published. It features messages from the Principal, the manager of the college, and other persons of repute.

In-Sight

In-Sight focuses on the special events, workshops, and seminars of the month, alongside administrative and academic announcements of students' interest. It acts as the primary monthly newsletter keeping the campus informed and connected.

Scholarly Publications

Departmental e-Newsletters

Departments and faculties also bring out e-News Letters from time to time highlighting various achievements of the departments, faculty members, and students. They cover workshops and seminars organized by the respective departments, as well as other information regarding employment and future prospects.

Epoch Making Study Center

The EPOCH Making Study Center provides extensive scholarly research and advanced study facilities on Gandhi and Nehru. It regularly publishes monograms, research papers, leaflets, and pamphlets.

Souvenirs owned special occasions, particularly whenever National and International Seminars are organized, are also published to mark and commemorate the occasion.
