Heritage

The College Museum

Step into the past with Virasat. Our museum meticulously curates an extensive collection of historical science artifacts, philosophical manuscripts, and study archives that trace the intellectual evolution of our institution and the wider scientific community.

HERITAGE
Tribal Heritage & Archival Photography

A significant portion of the Virasat archives is dedicated to rare ethnographic photography mapping the lives of indigenous tribes. These stunning visual records capture the rich cultural tapestry, traditional practices, and daily rhythms of ancient communities, preserved meticulously for anthropological study and historical appreciation.

FOSSILS
Prehistoric Fossil Remains

Our natural history collection boasts an invaluable array of prehistoric fossil remains. From ancient marine life imprints to terrestrial bone fragments dating back millions of years, these exhibits offer an incredible window into evolutionary biology and Earth's dynamic geological past, serving as a core resource for our science faculties.
