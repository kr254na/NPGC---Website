How It Works

Recruitment Process

1
Registration

Eligible students register with the placement cell. CV shortlisting and eligibility screening.

2
Pre-Placement Talk

Company representatives introduce roles, culture, and expectations to shortlisted students.

3
Aptitude / Test

Written aptitude, technical, or domain-specific assessments conducted on campus.

4
Interview & Offer

Technical and HR interviews, followed by offer letters issued to selected candidates.
