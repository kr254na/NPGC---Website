About Department

Department at a Glance

The Department of Economics at National P.G. College offers both B.A. and M.A. programmes, fostering strong academic foundations and advanced analytical skills. Committed to excellence in teaching and research, the department blends economic theory with empirical applications using modern econometric tools. Key areas of focus include income inequality, international remittances, fiscal policy, services trade, digital transformation, and environmental sustainability. Through panel discussions, special lectures, and academic activities, students engage with contemporary economic issues such as Union Budget analysis and global trade dynamics. The department prepares learners for higher studies, research, public policy, and diverse professional careers.

Expert Mentors

Dr. Ananya Dixit

Assistant Professor Ph.D.

Dr. Ananya Dixit is an accomplished academician and Assistant Professor of Economics at National P.G. College, Lucknow. She holds a Ph.D. from the University of Lucknow, with specialization in international trade in services and its implications for the Indian economy. Her scholarly contributions are reflected through publications in Scopus-indexed and UGC CARE-listed journals, along with presentations at reputed national and international conferences on contemporary themes such as digital economy, globalization, and sustainable development. A recipient of academic recognition for research excellence, she has qualified UGC-NET (Economics) and JRF (Commerce). With prior experience as a Senior Research Fellow and as a mentor for competitive examinations, Dr. Dixit is known for her research-driven, analytical, and student-centric teaching approach.

Mr. Santosh Yadav

Assistant Professor & HOD Ph.D.

Mr. Santosh Yadav is an Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Economics at National P.G. College, Lucknow—an Autonomous, CPE, and NAAC Grade ‘A’ institution affiliated with the University of Lucknow. He secured the 3rd rank in the examination conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Services Commission (2019). He is currently enrolled in the Ph.D. program at the University of Lucknow. He is the author of the book “Recent Advancements in the Field of Commerce and Economics & Its Application in Industries” (2022) and has published several research papers in reputed journals, including Economic & Political Weekly (2023). His academic interests include financial inclusion, taxation, and economic development policies. A dedicated teacher and researcher, he actively participates in faculty development programs, refresher courses, and national conferences, thereby contributing significantly to the growth of higher education in India.
