Ms. Srishti Sinha

Assistant Professor

Ms. Srishti Sinha is an academician in Political Science with specialization in Western Political Thought and International Relations. She holds a Master’s degree in Political Science from the University of Lucknow and has qualified the UGC-NET examination. She has submitted her doctoral research on Sub-regionalism in South Asia with special reference to BIMSTEC. With over three years of teaching experience, she is engaged in undergraduate and postgraduate instruction, with a focus on developing critical understanding of political theory and global affairs. Her areas of interest include regionalism, foreign policy, and contemporary international politics.
