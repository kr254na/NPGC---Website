How to Apply?

1

Registration (OTR)

Register yourself on the UP State Portal or National Scholarship Portal using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.

2

Online Form Filling

Fill in your academic, personal, and bank details carefully. Upload all required documents as per size limits.

3

Final Submission & Printout

Verify all details before "Final Submission". Take a hard copy printout of the full application form.

4

College Verification

Submit the hard copy along with all supporting documents to the College Scholarship Cell for institutional verification.

Facing Technical Issues?
