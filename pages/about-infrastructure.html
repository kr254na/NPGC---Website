Campus Infrastructure Overview

Academic Growth

Faculty of Science, Management Studies, and Computer Science were introduced with required cutting-edge laboratories to keep pace with growing academic demands.

Major Additions

Immense additions include an 800-seater auditorium, Evaluation & Secrecy cells, Administrative block, Central Library, underground parking, girls' hostel, and sports grounds.

Student Facilities

Every academic block features dedicated office and common rooms, with separate well-maintained common rooms and washrooms for female students, male students, and staff.

Inclusivity & Access

Fully accessible campus with elevators and ramps. Special facilities include audio books, screen-readers, braille displays, and specialized support for dyslexic and hearing-impaired students.

Major Facilities

Central Library

The college boasts a fully automated central library that serves as a hub of knowledge and academic excellence. It houses a vast collection of thousands of books across diverse disciplines, along with a wide range of national and international journals. In addition to its rich physical resources, the library provides access to numerous e-resources, including e-books, online journals, and digital databases, enabling students and faculty to stay updated with the latest information and research. Equipped with modern infrastructure and user-friendly systems, the library ensures a seamless and efficient learning experience for all its users.

Sports Facilities

The college offers excellent sports and fitness facilities, including well-maintained playgrounds, a spacious indoor sports hall, and a fully equipped gymnasium. These facilities encourage students to maintain an active and healthy lifestyle while nurturing their athletic talents. The institution also regularly organizes and participates in inter-college tournaments, providing students with opportunities to showcase their skills, build teamwork, and develop a spirit of sportsmanship and healthy competition.

Student Hostel

The college provides a secure and comfortable hostel facility for girls, designed to ensure a safe and supportive living environment. The hostel is equipped with round-the-clock CCTV surveillance and reliable security systems, offering peace of mind to both students and their families. With high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity and modern amenities such as well-furnished rooms, hygienic dining facilities, and common recreational areas, the hostel ensures a convenient and homely atmosphere that supports both academic focus and overall well-being.

ICT & Smart Classrooms

The college is committed to integrating technology into the learning process through its ICT and Smart Classrooms. These modern educational spaces are equipped with advanced audio-visual aids, including projectors, interactive smart boards, and sound systems, creating an engaging and dynamic learning environment. The campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity ensures seamless access to digital resources and online learning platforms. These facilities support innovative teaching methodologies, facilitate interactive learning, and enable students to engage with digital content effectively, preparing them for a technology-driven future.
