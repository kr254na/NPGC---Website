About Department

Department at a Glance

The Department of Journalism and Mass Communication at National P.G. College is dedicated to nurturing socially responsible media professionals equipped with critical thinking, ethical values, and practical media skills. The department offers a comprehensive curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on training in print, broadcast, and digital media. Our focus extends beyond traditional journalism to include new-age media, advertising, public relations, and media research. We emphasize hands-on experience through our well-equipped studios, editing labs, and college publications. The department also promotes interdisciplinary learning and industry exposure through internships, workshops, and guest lectures by media professionals. With a commitment to academic excellence and professional development, we prepare students for successful careers in the dynamic field of media and communication.
