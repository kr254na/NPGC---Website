Centre for Open & Distance Learning

The Distance Learning Centre (DLC) at National Post Graduate College is an authorized study centre for two of India's premier open universities:

  • IGNOU - Indira Gandhi National Open University
  • UPRTOU - Uttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tandon Open University, Allahabad

Our centre offers a wide array of programs including certificate, diploma, and degree courses designed for working professionals and students seeking flexible learning paths.
