About Department

Department at a Glance

The Department of Psychology at National P.G. College, Lucknow, is dedicated to the scientific study of the mind and behavior. Our faculty members are experts in various subfields of psychology, including clinical psychology, counseling psychology, cognitive psychology, and social psychology.

Dr. Nirja Singh

Head of Department

HOD's Message

Psychology is the science of behavior and mental processes. It seeks to understand how people think, feel, and behave, both individually and in groups. Our department is committed to providing students with a comprehensive education in psychology, including its history, theories, research methods, and applications.

Expert Mentors

Mr. Sakchham Shukla

Assistant Professor

Sakchham Shukla is an Assistant Professor at Department of Psychology, pursuing Ph.D from University of Lucknow , qualified in both NET and GATE exams, with a strong academic orientation toward teaching, research, and scholarly development. His areas of interest include psychological immunity, depression, suicidal ideation, and mental health from social and gender perspectives. He is actively involved in academic writing and research, contributing to contemporary psychological discourse. He adopts a student-centered and evidence-based teaching approach, aiming to foster analytical thinking and conceptual clarity. Committed to academic excellence, he strives to create an intellectually stimulating learning environment while promoting mental health awareness and advancing psychological research.
