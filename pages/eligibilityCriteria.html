Important Notes for Candidates

Merit Preparation: For PG courses, the merit list is prepared based on marks from High School, Intermediate, and Graduation.

Entrance Exams: Admission to BBA, BCA, and B.Voc programs is through entrance tests like LUACMAT or LUACCET.

Document Verification: Eligibility will be strictly verified at the time of counselling/admission. Incorrect information may lead to cancellation.
