Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Meritum Ethicus (Merit with Ethics)

Social, regional, territorial, geographical, racial and cultural boundaries no longer hold relevance in the present scenario. Merit and ethics are the two pillars upon which the foundation of the academic programmes of the College rests.

The college is committed to instill in the students a deep sense of ethics, which having amalgamated with their inner talents transforming them into ideal, dedicated, sincere and responsible citizens of the world.

Our Mission

The mission of the college is focused on the overall development of the individuals who can serve their organizations, their society and humanity at large with dedication.

We are determined to provide an environment where the students can flexibly develop their personality and bring out the perfection bestowed upon them by nature, ensuring each student acquires in-depth knowledge in his chosen discipline.
