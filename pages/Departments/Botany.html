HOD's Message

Welcome to the Department of Botany, where we explore the fascinating world of plants and their vital role in sustaining life on Earth. Our department is committed to providing students with a comprehensive understanding of plant science, from cellular biology to ecology. Through hands-on research and fieldwork, we empower students to become future botanists, conservationists, and environmental stewards.
