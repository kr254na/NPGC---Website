Hyperlinking Guidelines

Last Updated: March 2026  |  National Post Graduate College, Lucknow

1. Overview

This Hyperlinking Policy governs the linking of external websites to npgc.in (the official website of National Post Graduate College, Lucknow) and the linking from our website to external third-party websites. NPGC strives to maintain high standards of transparency and accuracy in all hyperlinks published on or referencing our website.

2. Linking to Our Website

Other websites may link to the home page of npgc.in provided that:

  • The link is not used in a misleading, defamatory, or unlawful context.
  • The link does not imply any endorsement, affiliation, or partnership with NPGC that does not exist.
  • The link does not frame the NPGC website within another website in a manner that misrepresents the source of the content.
  • The linked website does not contain content that conflicts with the values and standards of NPGC.

NPGC reserves the right to withdraw linking permission at any time without prior notice.

3. Links from Our Website to External Sites

The NPGC website may contain hyperlinks to external websites for the convenience of its users. These links are provided in good faith and for informational purposes only. The inclusion of a link does not constitute:

  • An endorsement or recommendation of the linked website or its content by NPGC.
  • Any affiliation or association between NPGC and the operators of the linked website.
  • A guarantee of the accuracy, reliability, or completeness of the information on the external site.

NPGC is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or availability of external websites.

4. Government & Regulatory Links

Links to official government and regulatory websites (such as the Ministry of Education, UGC, NAAC, University of Lucknow, and State Scholarship Portal) are provided to assist users in accessing authentic public-sector information. NPGC does not control these platforms and cannot guarantee that the information therein is current or complete.

5. Broken or Outdated Links

NPGC endeavors to keep all hyperlinks on this website active and functional. If you encounter a broken or outdated link, please notify us at support@npgc.in with the page URL and the nature of the issue so that it can be corrected promptly.

6. Social Media Links

Links to NPGC's official social media profiles (LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, Instagram) are provided on this website. Users accessing these platforms are subject to the terms and privacy policies of the respective social media platforms. NPGC is not responsible for content generated by third parties on these platforms.

7. Contact Us

For permissions or queries related to this Hyperlinking Policy, contact: support@npgc.in
