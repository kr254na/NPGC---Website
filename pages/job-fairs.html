Be Prepared

How to Prepare

1
Register Early

Sign up through the placement cell before the deadline. Registered students get a fair kit, entry pass and priority queue for popular company stalls.

2
Polish Your Resume

Bring 10–15 printed copies of your updated resume. The placement cell offers free resume review sessions in the week before every fair.

3
Research Companies

Study participating companies, roles and expectations beforehand. Arrive knowing which stalls matter most to you.

4
Dress & Arrive Early

Dress professionally, arrive at 9 AM sharp and approach each company stall confidently. On-the-spot offers are given throughout the day.
