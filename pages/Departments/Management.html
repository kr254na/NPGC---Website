About Department

Department at a Glance

The Department of Management at National P.G. College is dedicated to nurturing socially responsible business leaders equipped with critical thinking, ethical values, and practical management skills. The department offers a comprehensive curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on training in business administration, digital business, and management research. Our focus extends beyond traditional management to include new-age business practices, entrepreneurship, and corporate strategy. We emphasize hands-on experience through our well-equipped computer labs, business simulation software, and industry interaction programs. The department also promotes interdisciplinary learning and industry exposure through internships, workshops, and guest lectures by industry professionals. With a commitment to academic excellence and professional development, we prepare students for successful careers in the dynamic field of management.

She is an experienced academician in the field of Management ,having an experience of more than18 years with a strong focus on Business Enviornment,Retail Management, Consumer Behaviour, and Sustainable Business Practices.
