1. Ownership of Content

All content published on the official website of National Post Graduate College (npgc.in), including but not limited to text, articles, photographs, graphics, logos, icons, button images, data compilations, and software, is the exclusive intellectual property of NPGC or its content suppliers and is protected by applicable Indian and international copyright laws.

2. Permitted Use

Users are permitted to:

  • Access and view content on the website for personal, non-commercial, and informational purposes.
  • Download or print a single copy of any page for personal reference only.
  • Share website links for non-commercial informational purposes with appropriate attribution to NPGC.

3. Prohibited Use

Without express written permission from NPGC, users are prohibited from:

  • Reproducing, duplicating, copying, or reselling any material from this website for commercial purposes.
  • Modifying, adapting, or creating derivative works from NPGC content.
  • Redistributing content on any platform without acknowledgment of NPGC as the source.
  • Using NPGC's name, logo, or branding materials without prior written authorization.
  • Scraping, data harvesting, or mirroring the website in any form.

4. Academic Content

Study materials, syllabi, research papers, examination papers, and publications made available on this website are the intellectual property of NPGC and/or the University of Lucknow. These materials may be used for personal academic purposes only. Any commercial use, reproduction, or redistribution without prior written permission is strictly prohibited.

5. Third-Party Content

Certain content displayed on this website may belong to third parties (e.g., government regulations, UGC circulars, and affiliated university documents). Such content is reproduced for informational purposes only and all rights remain with the respective owners. NPGC does not claim ownership over third-party content.

6. Copyright Infringement

If you believe that any content on our website infringes upon your copyright, please notify us at support@npgc.in with the following details: (a) identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed; (b) identification of the infringing material with sufficient information to locate it; (c) your contact information.

7. Contact Us
