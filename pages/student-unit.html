Youth Empowerment

Rovers & Rangers

"True education is the one that builds character, confidence, and a sense of responsibility."

In todays dynamic world, education goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. The holistic development of students physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually has become essential. In this context, the Rover-Ranger Unit under The Bharat Scouts and Guides serves as a powerful platform for nurturing well-rounded individuals.

National Post Graduate College, an esteemed institution affiliated with University of Lucknow, actively promotes such initiatives to empower its students beyond academics. Any student of the college, irrespective of their stream, can become a part of this unit for a typical duration of 3 years.

Physical Development

Building stamina and physical resilience through regular training.

Mental Strength

Enhancing focus, discipline, and emotional intelligence.

Social Awareness

Developing empathy and community service mindset.

Spiritual Enrichment

Exploring values and ethics for holistic growth.
