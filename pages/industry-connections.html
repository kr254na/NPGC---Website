Why It Matters

Benefits of Partnerships

Priority Placement Access

MOU partners commit to visiting NPGC for campus drives before conducting open-market hiring, giving our students a first-mover advantage.

Exclusive Internships

Several MOUs include dedicated internship slots reserved exclusively for NPGC students — often with stipends and PPO conversion possibilities.

Subsidised Certifications

Partner organisations offer discounted or free certification programmes, training courses and digital badges valued by industry recruiters.

Expert Guest Lectures

Industry partners regularly conduct guest sessions, workshops and panel discussions on campus, providing real-world knowledge and exposure.

Research & Projects

Select MOUs open doors to collaborative research, live industry projects and case study competitions that strengthen student portfolios.

Long-Term Relationships

Formal MOUs create trust and accountability, ensuring consistent year-on-year engagement rather than one-time interactions with industry partners.

How It Works

MOU Process

1
Identify Partner

The placement cell identifies industry bodies, companies or institutions aligned with NPGC's academic programmes and student needs.

2
Negotiate Scope

Both parties discuss and agree on the scope — placements, internships, training, research, guest lectures or a combination thereof.

3
Sign the MOU

A formal signing ceremony is held at NPGC with the Principal, Manager, Placement Head and partner representatives.

4
Implement & Renew

Activities begin as per the agreed scope. MOUs are reviewed annually and renewed or upgraded based on outcomes and mutual interest.
