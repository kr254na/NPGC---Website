How to Enrol

1
Check Notice Board

Workshop announcements are posted on the NPGC placement board and shared via WhatsApp/email.

2
Register & Pay

Fill the form with Roll No. and pay the nominal fee (if applicable) at the placement cell.

3
Attend & Participate

Attend sessions, complete assignments and actively engage with faculty and activities.

4
Collect Certificate

Receive your certificate from Imarticus/NPGC after successful completion.
