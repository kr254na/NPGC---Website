Expert Mentors

Dr. Ananya Dixit

Assistant Professor Ph.D.

Dr. Ananya Dixit is an accomplished academician and Assistant Professor of Economics at National P.G. College, Lucknow. She holds a Ph.D. from the University of Lucknow, with specialization in international trade in services and its implications for the Indian economy. Her scholarly contributions are reflected through publications in Scopus-indexed and UGC CARE-listed journals, along with presentations at reputed national and international conferences on contemporary themes such as digital economy, globalization, and sustainable development. A recipient of academic recognition for research excellence, she has qualified UGC-NET (Economics) and JRF (Commerce). With prior experience as a Senior Research Fellow and as a mentor for competitive examinations, Dr. Dixit is known for her research-driven, analytical, and student-centric teaching approach.
