Expert Mentors

Dr. Nirmal Srivastava

Assistant Professor Ph.D.

Dr. Nirmal Srivastava is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics, associated with National P.G. College, Lucknow. He possesses extensive experience in higher education, research in fluid mechanics, and the application of mathematical sciences. He awarded Ph.D. (Mathematics) form University of Lucknow, Lucknow in year 2016. He was also associated as research project fellow in UGC Major Research Project during 2011 to 2014. He has over a decade of teaching and research experience. He has published 16 research papers in national and international journals, focusing in the field of Fluid Mechanics, Graph Theory and Mathematical Functions. Additionally, he has presented his work at numerous national and international seminars and conferences, including lectures on the Applications of Fluid Mechanics in Real Life. He is Life member of Anusandhan: Vigyaan Shodh Patrika, International Scientific Journal and Bharta Ganita Parishad.
